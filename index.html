    Help & about

    What it is for

    Project Phase Tracker is a lightweight way to track projects as they move through Idea → Build → Fix → Done.

    What it does not do

    It does not provide accounts, cloud sync, or team collaboration.

    Where data is stored

    Projects are saved locally in your browser (localStorage) and can be exported/imported as JSON.

    Privacy

    Data stays in this browser; Export/Import is the backup and transfer mechanism.
